About the author

Chinedu Philip Onoyima is the author of The Wind of Prairie and The Queer East. He is currently writing the epic fantasy A Wedding of Two Swords. His work explores power, identity, and the landscapes of home.

Quick facts

  • Works: The Wind of Prairie, The Queer East
  • Current project: A Wedding of Two Swords
  • Interests: worldbuilding, politics, cultural memory
